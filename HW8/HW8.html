Homework 8

This map shows Louisiana's Major Urban Areas in relation to its waterways. The urban areas have two buffers, a 1 kilometer buffer and a 5 kilometer buffer, that can show the potential impact that runoff from these urban areas can have on these waterways from just day to day pollution or major storm events.

In order to answer my question of how runoff from urban areas might affect Louisiana waterways, I had to create two buffers, one for a fairly typical day (1 km) and one that might represent a major storm event that would increase runoff (5 km). I also altered the transparency of each of urban area layers (original and buffer) so you can see how they compare to one another. I also had to change the projection of the original files to 3451, a Louisiana state projection.
